Say you want to work out and you search for some workout playlists, but since you are quite an elitist you do not want to stray far from your music comfort zone. Context Zone is a web app that creates personalized playlists, that aspire to convey the feeling you get from certain music, but at the same time contain songs you will find more familiar to your taste. Providing search keywords you are presented with relevant Spotify playlists (in descending popularity order). You can choose up to 10 playlists, whose songs' audio features are used to get a distribution that will be used to score the tracks familiar to your taste. Afterwards, you can choose between a shallow or deep profiling (how far away from your comfort zone you want to be) and a Spotify playlist is created for you. You can listen to it here or move to Spotify. Spotify access is required to get your top artists and to create your private playlist.
